# Domain Glossary

## Disaster

A natural disaster context that groups missions, urgency, logistics demand, and available operators.

## Logistics Company

An organization capable of executing humanitarian delivery operations for a selected disaster.

## Coordinator

A legal representative of a logistics company who registers the organization, publishes an operational plan, and manages operators.

## Operator

A field worker who performs last-mile delivery and captures delivery evidence.

## Mission

A funded humanitarian operation associated with one disaster and one logistics company.

## Donation

The donor-facing term for an economic contribution.

## Funding Commitment

A donor's confirmed contribution associated with a mission and governed by release conditions.

## Escrow

The smart-contract mechanism that locks funds and releases them only when mission proof conditions are met.

## Operational Advance

Optional early capital made available before final settlement.

## Margin Transfer

The amount transferred after validated delivery and deduction of any operational advance.

## Delivery Event

A field event representing a delivery attempt or completed handoff.

## Evidence Bundle

The minimum proof package attached to a delivery event, typically including operator ID, lot ID, QR result, timestamp, GPS, and optional note or photo.

## Validation Decision

The result of semantic evaluation over an evidence bundle: `accepted`, `requires_review`, or `rejected`.

## Timeline Event

A user-visible state change that explains what happened and when.

## Settlement

The financial closure triggered after a validated delivery event.

## GenLayer Analysis

An AI-assisted explanation of logistics company capability, trust level, risks, or evidence plausibility.

## Sync Queue

Client-side offline storage of delivery events awaiting transmission.
