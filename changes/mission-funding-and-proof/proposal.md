# Proposal: Mission Funding and Proof

## Intent

Implement the main donor-facing MVP slice that links disaster selection, company evaluation, funding commitment, escrow, and delivery proof into one understandable mission flow.

## In Scope

- donor flow `D-01` to `D-05`
- mission selection by disaster and logistics company
- wallet contribution tied to a mission
- escrow-backed funding commitment
- donor timeline visibility for proof and settlement milestones

## Out of Scope

- deep Aave integrations
- advanced dispute governance beyond review states

## Actor and Screen Coverage

- `Donor`
- `D-01`, `D-02`, `D-03`, `D-04`, `D-05`

## Success Criteria

- a donor can move from disaster selection to funding confirmation
- every funding commitment is attached to a mission and visible in timeline context
- post-funding states are readable without blockchain jargon
